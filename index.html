About us

MIT-Aarohan was started in the year 2014 by a group of passionate individuals of MIT College of Engineering. It boasts of students who have achieved excellence in the field of academic and organizations. Our sincerity, passion and commitment towards our goal have been the key towards our accomplishments.

MIT-Aarohan, the annual cultural festival of MIT College of engineering has grown into being Puneâ€™s largest and most sought after festival. What sets MIT-Aarohan apart from other festivals is the dedication of the students working for it and the urge to deliver the maximum.

The Events & Competitions lie in around 15 myriad events, most of which involve cash prizes and other goodies. Along with various cultural competitions such as group dance, solo singing, band wars and fine arts we also include events like LAN gaming and numerous sports events.

Being a young and vibrant festival, MIT-Aarohan makes it a point to target the youth with social issues .With initiatives such as blood-donation drive, interaction with old age home, tree plantation, education for all and library initiative, we consider this as our responsibility towards the betterment of the society.
